Icecream

What is Icecream? - Icecream is a delicious cold treat. It comes in so many different flavors and it is made with sweetened and flavored milk fat. It typically comes in a smooth consistency and it often contains gelatin, eggs, fruits, and nuts.

Flavors

<6>Icecream comes in many different flavors such as...

  4. Mint Chocolate Chip
  5. Cookie Dough
Compare Icecream vs. Gelato
Iceceam is airier and has a higher fat Gelato is softer and filled with flavor
